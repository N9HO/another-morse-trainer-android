## Full description

Learn to copy Morse code (CW) by ear — the way real operators do.

Another Morse Trainer teaches you with the proven Koch method: it starts with
two characters and adds the next one only when you're ready, building real
instant recognition instead of counting dots and dashes.

WHAT'S INSIDE
• Characters — the Koch-method ladder through A–Z and 0–9.
• Common Words — copy whole ham-radio words at speed.
• Abbreviations & Q-Codes — the CW shorthand you'll hear on the air.
• Pileup Runner — call CQ and pull callsigns out of a simulated pileup.
• Code Exam — the classic FCC/ARRL-style copy test at 5, 13, or 20 WPM.
• Listen & Learn — hands-free, eyes-free practice: hear the code, then the
  answer spoken aloud. Keeps playing with the screen locked, so you can learn
  while walking or driving.

BUILT FOR REAL PRACTICE
• Adjustable character speed and Farnsworth spacing.
• Adjustable sidetone pitch.
• Speak your answers instead of tapping them (optional, uses the microphone).
• A daily practice reminder to keep your streak alive.
• Progress tracking: practice streak, accuracy, your fastest copy, and a
  per-character recognition-speed chart so you can see exactly which characters
  are slowing you down.

No accounts. No ads. No tracking. Everything stays on your device.
