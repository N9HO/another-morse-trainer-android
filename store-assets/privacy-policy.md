# Privacy Policy — Another Morse Trainer

**Effective date:** June 13, 2026

Another Morse Trainer ("the app") is a Morse code learning app. This policy
explains what data the app handles. The short version: **the app does not
collect, transmit, or share any personal data, and has no accounts, ads, or
analytics.**

## Information the app stores

The app stores the following **only on your device**, in the app's private
storage:

- Your settings (code speed, Farnsworth spacing, sidetone pitch, haptics,
  reminder time, voice-answer preference).
- Your practice progress (daily streak, session history, per-character
  recognition times).

This information never leaves your device, is not sent to the developer or any
third party, and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

## Microphone

The app requests microphone access **only** for the optional "Voice answers"
feature, which lets you speak your answer instead of tapping it. When you use
this feature, your speech is transcribed using Android's built-in speech
recognition. Depending on your device, Android may process that audio with its
system speech‑recognition provider (for example, Google) to convert it to text.

The app itself does **not** record, save, or transmit any audio. The microphone
is used only while you actively tap "Speak answer," and you can leave this
feature turned off (it is off by default).

## Notifications

If you enable the daily practice reminder, the app schedules a local
notification on your device. No notification data is sent anywhere.

## Children

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal
information from anyone, including children.

## Third parties

The app contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third‑party tracking SDKs.
